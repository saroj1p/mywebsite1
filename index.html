Research Experience

PhD Candidate, Department of Physics, IIT Madras (Jan 2019 - Mar 2025)

  • Demonstrated room temperature, twist angle independent, momentum direct interlayer excitons (IXs) in van der Waals heterostructures of PbI2 and bilayer WS2 with long lifetimes (~100 ns) and spectral tunability (1.70–1.45 eV). (Nano Lett., 2024)
  • Studied stacking- and layer-parity-dependent photoluminescence in ReS2, showing distinct excitonic signatures for AA and AB phases. (Adv. Opt. Mat., 2025)
  • Fabricated MoO2/MoS2 core-shell nanowire memristors with volatile and nonvolatile switching. (Small, 2024)
  • Developed MATLAB tools for automated cryogenic transport studies revealing resistive switching in 2D memristors. (Appl. Phys. Lett., 2024)
  • Designed custom 2D material transfer systems for ambient and glovebox conditions with 90% yield.

MSc Research, Sri Sathya Sai Institute of Higher Learning (Jun 2016 - Apr 2018)
